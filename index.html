RULEs

objectives

Be the first player to connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).

how to play

Players must alternate turns, and only one disc can be dropped in each turn. The game ends when there is a 4 in a (row | column | diagonal) or a stalemate.
